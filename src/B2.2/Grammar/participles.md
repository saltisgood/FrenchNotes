# Participles

## Participe présent

- Formed with present indicatif plural first person stem + -ant
- Doesn't accord with the noun
- Has some consequence afterwards
- Can be replaced with "qui ..."
- Mostly used for formal speech and writing
- La femme mangeant la pomme vient de visiter le supermarché

## Adjectif verbal

- Formed in the same way as the participe présent
- Agrees with the noun
- Doesn't have to have an object/ consequence
- Sometimes the form differs a bit from the participe présent
  - fatiguant (participe) / fatigant (adjectif)
- L'homme ignore la femme courante

## Participe composé

- Participe présent du verbe "avoir" ou "être" and the participe passé du verbe
- It expresses a cause from the past
- Agrees with the noun when the auxiliary is être
- Pronouns go before the auxiliary verb
- La femme ayant visité le supermarché mange la pomme
- La famme etant allée au supermarché achete une pomme
